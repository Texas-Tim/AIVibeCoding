# Sprite Assets

Place your 8-bit sprite files here:

- player-red.png (16x16 pixels) - Red team player sprite
- player-blue.png (16x16 pixels) - Blue team player sprite  
- frisbee.png (8x8 pixels) - Frisbee sprite
- field-marker.png (optional) - Field markers

Recommended format: PNG with transparent background
Recommended size: 16x16 pixels for players, 8x8 for frisbee
Color palette: Use 8-bit style colors for authentic retro look
